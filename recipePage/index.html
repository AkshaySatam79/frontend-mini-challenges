Scrambled Egg Recipe

This recipe is for a basic, fluffy scrambled eggs, a quick and versatile meal.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 3-4 minutes
  • Servings: 1 serving (using 2 large eggs)

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk or water (optional, for fluffiness)
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs:Crack the eggs into a medium bowl. Add the milk (or water), salt, and pepper. Whisk the mixture until it is completely blended and uniform in color.
  2. Heat the pan:Melt the butter in a small non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cook the eggs:Pour the egg mixture into the hot skillet. Let the eggs cook for a few seconds without stirring until the edges begin to set.
  4. Scramble:Use a rubber spatula to gently pull the cooked eggs from the edges to the center of the pan, allowing the uncooked egg to flow to the bottom.
  5. Finish cooking:Continue cooking over medium-low heat, folding and stirring occasionally, until the eggs are mostly set but still glisten with moisture. Do not overcook, as they will continue cooking off the heat.
  6. Serve:Remove the pan from the heat immediately. Garnish with chives or parsley if desired, and serve immediately.
